What's new?

  • Selected tabs are now grouped

    • Tabs that are snoozed together, stay together.

  • Keyboard shortcuts in the popup

    • They've actually been around for quite a while. Hover over the keyboard icon in the popup to become rapid .

  • Duplicate sleeping tabs

    • Use the new overflow menus on each sleeping tab to duplicate it and choose a new time.

  • Hide sections in the nap room

    • Don't want to see a fat history section every time you're in the nap room? Hide away
